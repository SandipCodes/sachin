About Me

Born on 9th September, 1992 to a family of farmers in Ieet,Tq-Bhoom,Osmanabad, Shri Sachin Humbe has always been keenly aware of the systemic inefficiencies in the agricultural economy, Farmer Distress and Rural Development.

He saw the challenges his family faced first-hand and recognized early on that rural communities are often left out of the political process.

It was these experiences that ignited his passion for public service and strengthened his commitment to empowering farmers and finding a holistic solution to the agrarian crisis. He strongly believes that the plight of farmers should be more than a mere election plank. He is dedicated to pioneering ground-breaking rural development models that will drive inclusive growth and prosperity in the long term.

His political philosophy, inspired by CHATRAPATI SHIVAJI MAHARAJ , is a balance between the top-down and bottom-up approach to governance. Shri Sachin Humbe believes that the best legislation is born from a thorough understanding of grass-root issues and should reflect the needs of ordinary people, not just those of powerful interest groups. He sees great value in gathering insights and ideas from citizens. This has both helped him understand their socio-economic priorities and evaluate the roadblocks to policy implementation in their communities.
